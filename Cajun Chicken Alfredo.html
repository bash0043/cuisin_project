Cajun Chicken Alfredo

Category:Italian food

This recipe is adequate for 3 people.
Ingredients for cooking:
  • 150 gr boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 500 gr cooked fettuccine
  • 3 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1 cup blackening spice
  • ½ cup sliced scallions
  • ¾ cup grated parmesan
  • ¼ cup white wine
  • 3 tbsp minced garlic
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
Cooking Directions:
  1. (1)Heat oven to 350F.
  2. (2)Coat chicken with blackening spice and cook on high heat in a cast iron skillet. Bake in the oven for 10 minutes until the internal temp is 165F.
  3. (3)Slice chicken and set aside. Cook garlic and sun-dried tomatoes in a pan, then add chicken and white wine. Pour in heavy cream and reduce the sauce by half of them.
  4. (4)Add Parmesan, salt, pepper, and pasta to the sauce.
  5. (5)Place pasta on plates, pour sauce over pasta, and add scallions and Parmesan as garnish.

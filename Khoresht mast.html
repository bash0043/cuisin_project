Khoresht Mast

Category:Persian food

This recipe is adequate for 4 people.
Ingredients for cooking:
  • 400 gr Beef Neck
  • 1 Small Onion
  • 1 Egg Yolk
  • 5 Plain Yogurt
  • 1/2 Cup Sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon Saffron
Cooking Directions:
  1. Soak the saffron in boiling water for 30 minutes.
  2. Boil the beef in water with onion and turmeric until fully cooked.
  3. Shred and mash the beef with a hand blender.
  4. Mix egg yolk, sugar, and yogurt in a pot and heat over low heat while stirring until creamy.
  5. Add the mashed beef and saffron to the pot and stir until well blended.

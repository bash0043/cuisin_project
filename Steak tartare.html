Steak tartare

Category:French food

This recipe is adequate for 2 people.
Ingredients for cooking:
  • 1 kg of lamb meat, cut into cubes.
  • 3500 gr top sirloin, cleaned and trimmed
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup celery leaves, finely chopped and divided
  • 1/4 cup light olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, finely chopped and divided
  • 2 tablespoons small, brined capers, drained and unrinsed
  • 6 tablespoons finely diced shallots
  • 1 teaspoon freshly grated lemon zest
Cooking Directions:
  1. (1)Cut the steak into 1-inch cubes and park it in the freezer for 10 minutes.
  2. (2)Whisk together vinegar, dry mustard, and egg yolks in a bowl. Slowly stream in the oil while continuously whisking until emulsified. Whisk in shallots, capers, salt, and 2/3 of celery leaves and parsley.
  3. (3)Hand-chop meat or pulse in a food processor (in batches) for desired texture
  4. (4)Fold meat and dressing together with clean hands.
  5. (5)Plate using a pastry ring and garnish with reserved herbs and lemon zest.

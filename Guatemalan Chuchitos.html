Guatemalan Chuchitos

Category:Snacks

This recipe is adequate for 25 people.
Ingredients for cooking:
  • 2 bunches of dried corn cob husks.
  • 1 kilo of corn flour.
  • 1/4 kilo of melted vegetable shortening.
  • 2 teaspoons of salt.
  • 1 teaspoon of chicken broth.
  • 1 guac chili.
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin powder.
  • 1 teaspoon salt.
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper.
  • 1 kilo of pork meat in pieces.
  • 12 tomatoes
Cooking Directions:
  1. (1)Wash the husks well and leave them to soak to soften.Mix the flour with the butter, salt and the bouillon.
  2. (2)Add a little warm water to soften it, it should not be too watery.To make the errand , chop the tomatoes.
  3. (3)Wash and remove the seeds from the chili.Add a teaspoon of vegetable oil in a pan and fry the chili with the cumin.
  4. (4)Then, grind the tomato and chili together.It can be done in a shredder.Season with the salt and pepper.When it's cold, add the meat.
  5. (5)Place a little dough on the cob leaves.In the center add a tablespoon of recado and pieces of meat.Wrap the leaves and tie a strip of leaf or cooking thread at one end.Steam the chuchitos for about an hour and a half over low heat.

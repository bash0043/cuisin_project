Shishlik

Category:Persian food, BBQ

This recipe is adequate for 4 people.
Ingredients for cooking:
  • 1 kg of lamb meat, cut into cubes.
  • 3 big onions, chopped.
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced.
  • 5 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons olives oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon brewed saffron.
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
Cooking Directions:
  1. (1)In a large bowl, mix together the chopped onions, minced garlic, vegetable oil, olive oil, lemon juice, brewed saffron, salt, and black pepper.
  2. (2) Add the lamb cubes to the bowl and toss to coat them evenly with the marinade.
  3. (3) Cover and let the meat marinate for at least 8 hours in the refrigerator.
  4. (4)Once the meat is marinated, remove it from the refrigerator and let it come to room temperature. Preheat your grill to high heat.
  5. (5)Thread the meat onto skewers and grill for about 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until the lamb is cooked through and slightly charred on the outside.
